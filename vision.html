Long-Term Vision

"A living system where education, agriculture, and culture intersect."

ìkórè ilẹ̀ is not a restaurant. It is the first node in a larger ecosystem — one that feeds communities, teaches students, and returns people to the land.

📚
Education
School curriculum partnerships, culinary scholarship programs, and land-based learning initiatives that bring young people back to the source of food.
🌱
Agriculture
Co-owned community farms, direct farmer partnerships, and a reinvestment model that builds generational wealth in Black and immigrant agricultural communities.
🥁
Culture
Curated dinners, artist residencies, diaspora storytelling nights, and a permanent archive of West African and Mediterranean culinary heritage.
