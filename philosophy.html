Sourcing Philosophy

The Land
is the
Kitchen.

01
Proximity Over Convenience
Every protein, vegetable, and herb travels as short a distance as possible. We work directly with farms within 200 miles of Atlanta first — then regionally, then nationally.
02
Seasonal Integrity
Our menu is not a fixed document. It is a living response to what the land produces. No ingredient is forced out of season.
03
Community Agriculture
We co-invest in the farms that feed us. A portion of every cover goes directly toward land access programs for Black and immigrant farmers in the Southeast.
04
Culinary Lineage
Each dish carries a documented story: its cultural origin, the farm it came from, and the hands that grew it. Traceability is a form of respect.
