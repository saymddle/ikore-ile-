ìkórè ilẹ̀ Supper Club

Sun, Smoke & Sea

July 2026  ·  25 Guests

A summer tasting menu exploring the intersection of West African and Mediterranean flavors through peak-season produce, coastal ingredients, live-fire cooking, and refined technique.

Welcome Bite Curiosity

Watermelon Tartare

Compressed Watermelon · Green Mango Brunoise · Green Shito · Toasted Benne · Lime Oil

A bright and unexpected opening that plays with the idea of tartare through fruit, acid, and spice. Sweet watermelon, tart green mango, herbaceous green shito, and toasted benne establish the flavor language of the evening.

First Course Precision

Crab & Corn Agnolotti

Sweet Corn Purée & Charred Corn Kernel Filling · Charred Corn Velouté · Palm Oil Emulsion · Corn Silk & Grains of Paradise Tuile

Handmade agnolotti celebrating peak July corn and sweet crab. Multiple expressions of corn create depth while grains of paradise adds a delicate floral spice that quietly foreshadows the courses ahead.

Intermezzo Refreshment

Hibiscus · Watermelon · Lime

An icy palate cleanser balancing floral hibiscus, ripe watermelon, and bright lime. A brief pause before the menu turns toward smoke and fire.

Main Course Theater

Wood-Grilled Snapper

Charred Okra · Smoked Yogurt · Pepper Soup Consommé

Snapper cooked over live fire and paired with charred okra and smoked yogurt. Finished with a crystal-clear pepper soup consommé poured tableside, capturing the essence of pepper soup through aroma, spice, and clarity.

Dessert Comfort

Burnt Honey Malva

Blackberry–Tamarind Compote · Chin Chin Soil · Burnt Honey Glaze

A warm interpretation of malva pudding balanced by tart blackberry and tamarind. Chin chin soil provides texture and nostalgia while burnt honey ties the dish together with deep caramel notes.
