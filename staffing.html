The restaurant operates Tuesday through Saturday from 5:30 PM to 10:30 PM and serves approximately 70 guests per evening, resulting in an estimated 21,840 annual covers.

The labor structure reflects the operational needs of a contemporary fine dining restaurant while supporting the restaurant’s commitment to scratch cooking, seasonal menu development, elevated guest service, and maintenance of an on-site garden program.

Specialized culinary positions ensure consistent execution of the menu, while cross-trained floater positions provide flexibility during peak periods and special events. The dedicated garden team reinforces the farm-to-table identity of the restaurant and contributes directly to ingredient quality and sustainability initiatives.

This staffing model is designed to support approximately 70 nightly covers while maintaining the hospitality standards expected within the Buckhead fine dining market.
